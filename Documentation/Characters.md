# Project Eclipse - Character Profiles

## 1. Protagonist: Dr. Gabriel Vance
- **Role:** Lead Investigator / Astronomer
- **Age:** 38
- **Background:** Analytical, pragmatic scientist haunted by a past laboratory failure. Relies on reason, but struggles as horror defies logic.
- **Gameplay Attributes:** Standard movement speed, moderate stamina, uses lantern/flashlight, carries inventory journal.

---

## 2. Supporting Characters

### Dr. Elena Rostova (NPC / Audio Logs)
- **Role:** Chief Astronomer at Blackwood Observatory
- **Status:** Missing (Communicates via recordings & radio fragments)
- **Motivation:** Unlocked the secret of the Eclipse signal; attempted to contain it before losing control.

---

## 3. Threat Entities & Enemies

### The Shadow Stalkers (Common Hazard)
- **Behavior:** Patrol dark corridors. React to sound and light beams.
- **Weakness:** Intense UV / Concentrated light stuns them temporarily.

### The Eclipse Sentinel (Boss / Stalker Enemy)
- **Behavior:** Unkillable persistent pursuer during Act II and III.
- **Mechanics:** Manifests when sanity drops below 25% or during eclipse pulses.
