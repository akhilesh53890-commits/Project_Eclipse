# Project Eclipse - Gameplay Mechanics Document

## 1. Player Mechanics
- **Movement:** Walking, Crouching, Sprinting (stamina limited).
- **Sanity Meter:**
  - Depletes when in total darkness, viewing entity manifestations, or experiencing supernatural phenomena.
  - Effects: Visual distortion, audio hallucination, controls drift.
  - Recovery: Standing near safe light zones, consuming sedatives.
- **Inventory System:** 8-slot grid inventory. Real-time access (game does not pause).

---

## 2. Lighting Mechanics
- **Dynamic Flashlight:**
  - Requires battery management.
  - Focus Beam (drains battery fast, stuns shadow entities) vs Wide Beam (general light).
- **Environmental Lights:** Candles, generator switches, UV lanterns.

---

## 3. Puzzle Design Principles
- **Clue Distribution:** Document notes, environmental visual cues, sound cues.
- **Example Puzzle (Rotunda Constellation Alignment):**
  - Player rotates focal rings on a telescope matching star maps found in the library to unlock the subterranean elevator.
