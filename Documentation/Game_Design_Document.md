# Project Eclipse - Game Design Document (GDD)

## 1. Executive Summary
- **Game Title:** Project Eclipse
- **Genre:** Psychological Survival Horror / Narrative Thriller
- **Target Audience:** Fans of atmospheric horror, environmental storytelling, and puzzle-solving (e.g., Silent Hill, Resident Evil, Amnesia).
- **Target Platforms:** PC (Steam), Consoles (PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S)
- **Engine:** Unity (2022.3 LTS / 6)
- **Camera Perspective:** First-Person / Over-the-Shoulder Third Person

---

## 2. High Concept & Core Vision
*Project Eclipse* immerses players in an isolated, decaying environment where reality fractures under the shadow of a celestial eclipse. The core gameplay balances tense exploration, dynamic sanity management, light-and-shadow puzzle mechanics, and psychological dread over jump scares.

---

## 3. Key Pillars
1. **Atmospheric Dread:** Immersive sound design, dynamic lighting, and environmental storytelling.
2. **Resource Scarcity & Survival:** Limited batteries, light sources, defensive items, and stamina.
3. **Psychological Sanity System:** Hallucinations and spatial shifts as sanity degrades.
4. **Intricate Environmental Puzzles:** Interconnected lock-and-key mechanics and celestial alignments.

---

## 4. World & Setting
- **Primary Location:** The Blackwood Observatory & Surrounding Mountain Complex.
- **Time Period:** Late 1990s.
- **Visual Style:** Dark, desaturated tones contrast with ethereal lunar/solar eclipse glows, heavy volumetric fog, vintage analog tech.

---
