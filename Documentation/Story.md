# Project Eclipse - Story & Narrative Outline

## 1. Plot Overview
During a rare total solar eclipse, communication with the Blackwood Astronomical Observatory goes dark. Dr. Gabriel Vance, a researcher sent to investigate, arrives to find the facility abandoned, doors sealed from the inside, and reality distorting around unusual celestial phenomena.

---

## 2. Story Acts

### Act I: The Darkened Threshold
- Gabriel arrives at Blackwood Observatory under an eerie blood-red twilight.
- Discovers strange diary entries detailing cosmic signals received weeks prior to the eclipse.
- Core Goal: Restore power to the main rotunda to access the lower research levels.

### Act II: Fractured Reality
- As the eclipse reaches totality, geometry inside the facility shifts.
- Gabriel encounters entity manifestations born from memories and repressed guilt.
- Core Goal: Collect three lens components needed to recalibrate the main celestial telescope.

### Act III: The Umbra Protocol
- Gabriel reaches the subterranean chamber beneath the observatory core.
- The truth behind the Eclipse Ritual and the fate of the observatory crew is uncovered.
- Climactic decision determining the ending.

---

## 3. Themes & Symbols
- **Light vs. Shadow:** Light is safety and truth, shadow is insanity and delusion.
- **The Eye / Eclipse:** Represents omnipresent observation and celestial horror.
- **Echoes of Memory:** Auditory and visual artifacts reflecting past events.
